## Design Context

### Users
The page is primarily for investors and startup customers evaluating Peter as a founder and the AI FinOps product direction. They are scanning for credibility, market clarity, technical depth, and evidence that the founder can execute.

### Brand Personality
Confident, curious, and founder-led. The interface should feel like a serious product-founder presence: sharp, credible, energetic, and still personal enough to support build-in-public writing.

### Aesthetic Direction
Move away from heavy retro pixel-art toward a refined product-founder portfolio. The page should feel closer to a polished startup founder profile than a developer novelty page. Keep subtle technical texture only where it reinforces credibility; avoid game-like styling as the dominant visual language.

### Design Principles
- Lead with founder credibility and product clarity in the first viewport.
- Make writing feel like evidence of momentum, not a secondary blog list.
- Use restrained technical details, not decorative developer tropes.
- Favor editorial confidence: strong hierarchy, readable prose, sharp spacing, and intentional asymmetry.
- Preserve fast static-site performance and responsive behavior.
